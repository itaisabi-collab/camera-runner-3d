Privacy Policy - RUN! 3D Camera Runner

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

RUN! 3D - Camera Runner ("the Game") is developed by Itaisa. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Game.

2. Camera Usage

The Game uses your device's camera for body tracking gameplay (MediaPipe Pose detection). Camera data is processed entirely on your device in real-time. We do not record, store, or transmit any camera footage or images to any server.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The Game does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to play.

  • Camera data: Processed locally, never transmitted
  • Game scores: Stored locally on your device only
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics services
  • No tracking: We do not track users

4. Advertising

The Game may display ads through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Camera processing is done entirely on-device.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob - Advertising (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • MediaPipe - On-device pose detection (no data transmitted)

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
